From Riga to Piņķi

  • By Bus:

    Buses run regularly from Riga to Piņķi.

    Check the bus schedule on the Rīgas satiksme website.

    Tickets can be purchased at Narvesen outlets, ticket machines, or via the Mobilly app.

  • By Train:

    There is no direct train to Piņķi, but you can take a train to a nearby station and then use local transport.

    Check the train schedules on Pv.lv.

From Jūrmala to Piņķi

  • By Bus:

    Buses run frequently from Jūrmala to Piņķi.

    For the latest bus schedules, visit the Rīgas satiksme website.

  • By Train:

    Similar to the route from Riga, there is no direct train to Piņķi, but you can take a train to a nearby station and then use local transport.

    Check the schedules on Pv.lv.

Additional Information

Public transport in Riga includes buses, trams, trolley-buses, and minibuses, operating from around 5 a.m. to midnight. Night buses are available on weekends.

Tickets for public transport should be registered with an electronic validator each time you board. For more information on ticket prices and purchasing options, refer to the Rīgas satiksme website.
